About

In this personal project, I utilized scenes where I interpreted the Calvin Klein perfume bottle. My aim was to create visually striking and unconventional images that resonate with the character of the perfume. The combination of purple and black colors helped to achieve an elegant and mysterious ambiance. During the post-processing stage, I used a grain effect to obtain renders that are slightly rough yet realistic in appearance.

Play

The Result

As a result, I created images with an elegant look, high contrast and most importantly, suitable for the character of the perfume and the brand.

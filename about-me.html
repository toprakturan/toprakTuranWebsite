Hello, I'm Toprak!

I've been working as a 3D artist for 1.5 years. Previously, I worked as a 3D Artist at Ratic for 10 months. I specialize in creating models from scratch, as well as producing photorealistic renders and realistic textures. My artistic approach is modern and up-to-date, and I always strive to ensure that my renders are of the highest quality in terms of lighting, background, and scenery. I take pride in my work and value teamwork. I enjoy taking initiative with my teammates and helping them with other processes.
